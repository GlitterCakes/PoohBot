Beer List

This is the list of beers that I've tried. Beers will be linked to their corresponding page on Beer Advocate (if it exists), and favorite beers will be marked with an asterisk. The beer list is also on my homenode at e2, though the list on this page is more accurate. In the paragraphs below I will try to correlate numbers with time periods and travels.

I started keeping the list in the spring of my senior year of college at TAMU. At that time I listed all of the beers that I could recall having tried. These are the first 31 beers on the list. Beers, with few a few exceptions, are listed chronologically beginning with 32, which also corresponds to the beer tasting hosted at my apartment on 29 March 2003 (32-36). A trip to see Superdrag in Little Rock saw the addition of four new beers (40-43). My goal was to try 100 beers before the summer of 2003 was up and grad school at MIT started. I saved number 100 to celebrate my move to Boston, and finished out the summer at 106.

Beers 107 to 148 comprise my first year at MIT. Summer of 2004 started with the Art of Beer Fest (149-157), continued with a trip to NYC (165-170), and a trip back to Texas (Waco, College Station, Houston; 185-193). I ended the summer with beer number 200, Unibroue Edition 2004.

A trip back to Texas for Brook & Brad's wedding (2 October 2004) yielded two new beers (202-204). Winter break 2004-2005 yielded 232-250. A weekend trip to New York to visit David (April 2005) yielded 265-268. Schwag, Jever & Dentergems were on tap at Schnäck in Brooklyn, and Zywiec at a Polish restaurant (S & B maybe?) in Brooklyn. Schwag's true identity is yet to be determined. I tried beers 302-307 during various visits to Cricket's over my trip home in December 2005 and January 2006.

Beers 319-326 were tried during a trip to NY in February 2006. The Extreme Beer Fest yielded 329-339. Another trip to NYC in February yielded 343. I tried at 358-363 NERAX 2006. A trip to NY for David's birthday yielded 369-374. Beers 406-421 were tried on a weekend trip to Cooperstown, NY for Belgium Comes to Cooperstown in July 2006.
